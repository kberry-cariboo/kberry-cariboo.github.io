Privacy Policy

1. Overview

CashFlow is a personal cash-flow forecasting and budgeting application developed by Kenneth Berry. The app is hosted on GitHub Pages at https://kberry-cariboo.github.io/. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is stored, and what choices you have.

2. Data Storage

All budget data you enter — including entries, categories, overrides, and preferences — is stored locally in your web browser using the browser’s localStorage API.

  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any external server.
  • No databases or back-end services are used.
  • Clearing your browser data will remove all locally stored CashFlow data.

3. Google Drive Integration (Optional)

CashFlow offers an optional Google Drive sync feature that allows you to save and load your budget data to your own Google Drive account. This feature uses the Google Drive API with the following scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file — allows CashFlow to create, read, and edit only files it creates (a single file named CashFlow_Data.json). CashFlow cannot access any other files in your Google Drive.

When you use Google Drive sync:

  • Authentication is handled entirely by Google’s Identity Services. CashFlow never sees, stores, or transmits your Google password.
  • An OAuth access token is held in memory only for the duration of your browser session. It is not saved to disk or localStorage.
  • No refresh tokens are stored. You must re-authenticate each session.

4. Information We Collect

CashFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used.
  • No cookies are set by the application.
  • No personal data is sent to any server.
  • No advertising networks or third-party services are integrated (other than Google Drive, if you opt in).

5. Third-Party Services

CashFlow loads the following third-party resources via CDN:

  • React & ReactDOM — UI framework (cdn.jsdelivr.net)
  • Recharts — charting library (cdn.jsdelivr.net)
  • Google Fonts — Inter font family (fonts.googleapis.com)
  • Google Identity Services — OAuth authentication (accounts.google.com)
  • Google API Client — Drive API access (apis.google.com)

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. CashFlow does not control or have access to any data these services may collect.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Persists in your browser until you clear it. You can delete all CashFlow data at any time by clearing your browser’s localStorage for this site.
  • Google Drive data: The CashFlow_Data.json file remains in your Google Drive until you delete it. You can revoke CashFlow’s access at any time at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

7. Children’s Privacy

CashFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
